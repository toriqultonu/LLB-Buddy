What notice period is required for terminating an employee under Bangladesh law?

Under Section 20 of the Bangladesh Labour Act 2006:

  • Monthly-rated workers: 120 days notice
  • Other workers: 60 days notice

This applies to workers with at least 1 year of continuous service. Payment in lieu of notice is also permitted.

What about compensation for termination?

For retrenchment, Section 20 also requires:

Compensation of 30 days' wages for each completed year of service, in addition to any notice pay.
